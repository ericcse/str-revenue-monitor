Pacing settings

Each row is a threshold: when LY final is at or above Final Occ. %, that row applies. From Wks to Lowest weeks before the stay: ramp Target → Lower Pctl % by Wks to Lower weeks out, then Lower → Lowest Pctl% by the first night. Set Wks to Lower to 0 or ≥ Wks to Lowest for one straight ramp Target → Lowest. 0 on Wks to Lowest = always Target. Drag the grip on a row to reorder; if two rows share the same Final Occ. %, the lower row in the table wins.
